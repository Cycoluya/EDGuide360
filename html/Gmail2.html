Gmail Account Guide

  1. Open Google Chrome, search CAO application and select "Apply"

  2. Select "Continue with Application"

  3. Enter your birth certificate/ ID number and click submit

  4. Select "Continue with Application"

  5. Fill in your personal details. If you don't have an email, check out the Gmail Guide to get help create a new Gmail account

  6. Enter your postal code and select the suburb/ area you live. If you don't have PO BOX number, select the "Street" address type and enter physical address details

  7. Enter your cellphone number and make sure to confirm the checkboxes before clicking next

  8. Select "Continue with Application"

  9. Save your CAO login and payment details. Select "Continue with Application"

  10. If you are not South African, Select "No" and choose your citizenship. Make sure to tick the Nsfas financial aid checkbox. Select "Yes" and enter your bank details if you have a bank account, select "No" if you don't have a bank account

  11. Provide your demographic details. Select your disability status and click next

  12. Enter your next of kin (someone you trust) personal and cellphone details

  13. Enter your parents/ guardian details and click next. Leave blank if you don't have any guardian and click next

  14. Search name of high school you attend or last attended. Add the correct school from the search results. Enter the year you started and you will complete/ completed at the school

  15. If you already completed your matric, select "Yes" and tick National Senior Certificate, enter your exam year and province. If you have not completed your matric, select "No" and click next

  16. Using the CAO Handbook, make sure you check the programme name, programme code, closing date, minimum points and requirents you need to have to be considered, and programme notes before choosing your desired programme

  17. Once you have chosen your desired programme from the CAO Handbook, enter the course code and click on add. Your programme will be displayed at the bottom of your screen

  18. Alternatively you can enter the programme name, select the institution/ university and search. Add your desired search results and it will be displayed at the bottom of your screen

  19. Once all 6 choices have been added, check all the highlighted information is correct and click next

    Note: 6 programme choices are required before you can continue with your application

  20. Review and check if all of your details are correct before submiting your CAO application

  21. If you wish to change certain information, there's an "Edit" option at the end of each application section

  22. Once you have checked all information is correct, click on "Process My Application" to send your CAO application

    Note: You will be required to upload all relevant supporting documents once your application has been submitted

  23. After submiting your CAO application, you should get an "Application successful" notification. An easy pay number will be available to make your cao application payment.

  24. To check your CAO application, go to CAO Home page and click on "Check my Application" link
